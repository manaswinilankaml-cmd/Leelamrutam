Long ago, in a small village, there lived a young girl named Meera who was a devoted follower of Goddess Durga.

Every morning before sunrise, Meera would light a lamp and pray to the goddess with all her heart.

One evening, while returning home from the forest, dark clouds gathered and the wind began to howl. Suddenly, a fierce tiger appeared on the path.

Meera was frightened, but she closed her eyes and prayed, “Mother Durga, please protect me.”

At that very moment, the tiger stopped moving. Behind Meera appeared a radiant vision of Goddess Durga, glowing with divine power.

The tiger slowly bowed its head and walked away peacefully.

When Meera opened her eyes, the forest was calm again.

From that day onward, the villagers believed even more strongly that the Divine Mother always protects those who call her with true devotion.
